La Senne Maritieme Diensten Belgium based company and it is a world class specialist marine products sales, and full range service support company serving the maritime market around EU & APAC since 2008.

Our technical service supports product groups, containing top industry brands, include navigation, dynamic positioning & sensors, naval, survey, subsea technology, navigation infrastructure together with marine engines, thrusters, gearbox, propulsion, hydraulics & Pneumatics’ system & Dry-docking supervisions & technical intelligence.

All La Senne Maritieme Diensten engineers, superiendents & manager are extensively trained and well experienced with product & services, EHS, WPSH, travel and work in challenging environments.
